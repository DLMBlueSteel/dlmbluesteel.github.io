BlueSteelAI Training Data

BlueSteelAI is a "Real AI" engine that intelligently distinguishes premium music from other YouTube media content.

Wikipedia Intelligence

  • BlueSteelAI strips your search query and silently scans Wikipedia to determine exactly what you searched for.
  • If Wikipedia describes it as an animated web series, video game, podcast, anime, or movie, the AI instantly categorizes it as Other Media.
  • If Wikipedia describes it as a song, album, or music video, the AI flags it as a Song and prepares for a premium upgrade.

iTunes Closeness Verification

  • When fetching premium metadata from iTunes, BlueSteelAI cross-references the iTunes Artist with the YouTube Channel to prevent incorrect matching (e.g., rejecting Katseye for SpongeBob).
  • It strictly verifies that any unique context words you typed in your search actually appear in the iTunes results before applying the premium metadata.
